Narendra Modi

Indian Prime-minister who changed the global diplomacy

Here's a time line of Narendra Modi:

  • 1950 - Born in Vadnagar, Bombay State, India.
  • 1968 - Leaves his family and went to the Ramakrishna Ashram in Almora.
  • 1970 - Lived with his uncle, working in the latter's canteen at the Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation
  • 1971 - Became a full-time pracharak (campaigner) for the RSS
  • 1978 - Received a Bachelor of Arts degree in political science from School of Open Learning at University of Delhi
  • 1983 - Received a Master of Arts degree in political science from Gujarat University.
  • 1985 - Was assigned by the RSS to the BJP in 1985
  • 1987 - Helped organise the BJP's campaign in the Ahmedabad municipal election, which the BJP won comfortably.
  • 1990 - Named a member of the BJP's National Election Committee
  • 1995 - Appointed as National Secretary of BJP.
  • 1998 - Appointed as BJP General Secretary.
  • 2001 - Became Chief Minister of Gujarat
  • 2002 - Won By-Elections in Gujarat and Second Term of Chief Ministerof Gujarat. Also Gujarat Riots happened.
  • 2007 - Third term of Gujarat Chief Minister
  • 2012 - Fourth Term of Gujarat Chief Minister
  • 2014 - Became 14th Prime Minister of Republic of India
  • 2019 - Second Term as Prime Minister of Republic of India
